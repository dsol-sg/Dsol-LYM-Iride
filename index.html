Iride is a wall lamp inspired by the world of furniture and the
shades of the eye. A large iris created with a 600 mm smoked glass plate
supported by a central metal disc, anchored to the wall through a 90 mm
spacer. Behind the metal disc there is a LED light source that emits up to
3700 Lumen, generating a pleasant indirect light without compromising the
light performances.
